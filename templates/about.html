About Us

Virtual Center

A Virtual Centre on Artificial Intelligence (AI)/ Machine Learning (ML)/ Deep Learning (DL) is established in IITM Pune in order to expand the domain through multidisciplinary programs in the field of Earth System Science.

This virtual Center will coordinate with the units of MoES to identify problems that can be addressed with AI/ML tools and will also plan and advise strategy to develop AI/ML based application tools.

This center will also be responsible for conducting seminars, workshops and other capacity building activities in the related areas.
